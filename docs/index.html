Instagram Follower Analyzer

See who doesn't follow you back. Paste in console, done. No app, no login, no tracking.

Your data never leaves your browser.
Or drag to bookmarks bar: IFA Bookmarklet
Dragging the bookmarklet lets you run it anytime from any tab.

How to use

1
Go to instagram.com and log in Make sure you're logged in to your Instagram account.
2
Open the browser console
Windows/Linux: F12 or Ctrl+Shift+J Mac: Cmd+Option+J
3
Paste the code and press Enter Click "COPY CODE" above, paste into the Console tab, and press Enter.
4
Click SCAN in the floating panel A panel appears in the top-right. Click SCAN and wait for results.

Features

Who doesn't follow back

See exactly who you follow that doesn't follow you back.

Bulk unfollow

Select and unfollow multiple users with built-in anti-ban delays.

Snapshots

Save scan results and compare over time to track follower changes.

Whitelist

Protect accounts you never want to accidentally unfollow.

Export

Download any list as CSV, TXT, or JSON.

No installation

Paste in console and go. Nothing to install or sign up for.

Built-in anti-ban protection

  • Random 800-1500ms delay between each scan request
  • 10-second pause every 10 scan requests (configurable)
  • 3-second delay between each unfollow (configurable)
  • 15-second pause every 10 unfollows (configurable)
  • Automatic exponential backoff on rate limit (HTTP 429)
  • Minimum cooldowns enforced - sliders can only increase values
